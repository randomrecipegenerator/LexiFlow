# LexiFlow — 2-Minute Demo Video Script

## Title: "From Lead to Retainer in 60 Seconds — LexiFlow Technologies Inc"

---

### Scene 1 (0:00–0:15) — The Problem
**Visual:** Split screen — left: attorney drowning in paperwork; right: missed call notification popping up.
**Narrator:** "Every day, law firms lose thousands in revenue — not because they lack clients, but because they can't respond fast enough. 80% of leads go cold within 5 minutes. And every hour spent screening intake is an hour not practicing law."

---

### Scene 2 (0:15–0:35) — The LexiFlow Intake Widget
**Visual:** Screen recording of `demo-intake.html` — the AI chat widget opens, a visitor types "I was in a car accident last week." The bot responds instantly, asks qualifying questions, shows a live score meter climbing from 45 → 82/100.
**Narrator:** "LexiFlow changes the game. Our AI Intake Agent embeds directly on your website — or runs as a 24/7 voice assistant. It captures every lead, asks the right questions, and *pre-qualifies* cases with the nuance of a senior attorney. Seconds after a visitor engages, they're scored, sorted, and ready for your team."

---

### Scene 3 (0:35–0:55) — AI Medical Chronologies
**Visual:** Screen recording of `demo-chronology.html` — toggling from "Raw Records" to "AI Chronology." The timeline expands with colored markers — "Legal Significance: Deviation from Standard of Care" highlighted in gold. Merit score shows 87/100.
**Narrator:** "Medical records are the most time-consuming part of case evaluation. LexiFlow's AI reads 5,000 pages of medical records and generates a trial-ready chronology in under 60 seconds. It flags deviations of care, identifies key witnesses, and calculates a merit score — turning weeks of paralegal work into minutes."

---

### Scene 4 (0:55–1:15) — Admin Dashboard
**Visual:** Panning across `dashboard.html` — showing the lead pipeline (New → Qualified → High Priority), the analytics charts (conversion rate, intake volume by channel, merit score distribution), and the "Sync to Clio" / "Sync to Filevine" buttons.
**Narrator:** "Everything flows into one unified dashboard. See your entire lead pipeline, qualification scores, medical merit reviews — all in real-time. With one click, sync qualified leads to Clio, Filevine, or LeadDock. No more manual data entry. No more spreadsheets."

---

### Scene 5 (1:15–1:35) — Team Collaboration & Settlement
**Visual:** Quick cuts — user clicking "Invite Team Member," user viewing the settlement estimator output ($142,500 predicted value), user attaching a medical chronology to a CRM record.
**Narrator:** "Invite your partners and paralegals. Each gets role-based access. Run settlement estimates against 50,000+ historical cases with 94% accuracy. Auto-generate demand letters. Your entire intake workflow — from first click to signed retainer — in one platform."

---

### Scene 6 (1:35–1:45) — The ROI
**Visual:** ROI Calculator overlay on pricing page — sliders animate to show 150 leads × $175/hr = $210,000 manual cost vs. $69/mo LexiFlow. "Save 99.9%" callout animates.
**Narrator:** "All six modules — AI Intake Agent, Voice AI Receptionist, Medical Chronologies, Document Drafting, DepoLens, and Settlement Estimator — for a flat $69 a month. No per-seat fees. No contracts."

---

### Scene 7 (1:45–2:00) — CTA
**Visual:** Final screen — LexiFlow logo with "Start Your 14-Day Free Trial" CTA button. Gold accent on navy background. Email link: `leads@lexiflow.co`.
**Narrator:** "Join 500+ firms already using LexiFlow to close cases faster and save thousands. Start your free trial today at lexiflow.co. LexiFlow — Enterprise Legal Intelligence."
