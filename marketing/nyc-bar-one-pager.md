### **Executive Summary**
LexiFlow is an enterprise-grade AI platform built specifically for plaintiff-side personal injury and medical malpractice firms. We automate the entire litigation lifecycle—from the first intake call to complex deposition analysis—enabling firms to scale without increasing headcount.

### **The LexiFlow 1.0 "Hardened" Suite**
*   **Voice AI Receptionist (24/7)**: HIPAA-compliant intake that qualifies leads in natural language, ensuring no high-value case is ever missed after hours.
*   **MeritScan™**: Transform a 1,000-page medical record into a citation-backed merit report in under 3 minutes. Validated against NY standard-of-care guidelines.
*   **DepoLens™ AI**: Real-time deposition intelligence. Detect contradictions between testimony and medical records instantly.
*   **Discovery-Vault™**: Secure, automated management of case-critical documents with defensive rendering for error-free review.
