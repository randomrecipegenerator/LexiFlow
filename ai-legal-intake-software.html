The Technical Distinction

Reasoning AI vs. Decision-Tree Bots

Most "AI" intake tools on the market today are not AI at all — they operate on pre-programmed if/then logic. LexiFlow is fundamentally different.

LexiFlow Reasoning AI

  • Uses an LLM fine-tuned on tens of thousands of real intake conversations
  • Trained on medical records, clinical terminology, and state-specific SOL requirements
  • Listens to the caller's full narrative in natural language
  • Extracts key case elements: date, facilities, injuries, opposing parties
  • Dynamically generates follow-up questions to probe liability, causation, and damages

Decision-Tree Bots

  • Pre-programmed if/then logic: "if auto accident, show Q1-5"
  • Cannot understand nuance or follow a caller's narrative
  • Breaks when caller says something the programmer didn't anticipate
  • Rigid menus, no adaptive questioning
  • No cross-referencing against medical records or case law
