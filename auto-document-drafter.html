Source Citation System

Every statement in a LexiFlow-drafted document includes a hyperlinked source citation. Click any citation to verify the source in seconds.

How Citations Are Generated

  1. Uploaded documents are indexed page-by-page with searchable vector embeddings
  2. The AI performs semantic retrieval to find the exact source passage
  3. Statements are only generated when a supporting source exists — no fabrication
  4. The citation is embedded as a hyperlink; clicking opens the source PDF at the exact page

Citation Format Example

“Ms. Rodriguez underwent an L4-L5 laminectomy on March 15, 2024, performed by Dr. James Chen at Mount Sinai West.”

[Source: Operative Report, Mount Sinai West, p. 3 — March 15, 2024]
