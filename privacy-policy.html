Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

1. Introduction

LexiFlow Legal Suite ("LexiFlow," "we," "us," or "our") provides an AI-powered legal intake and medical merit review platform for law firms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose information, including Protected Health Information (PHI), when you use our platform, website, and services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including HIPAA, HITECH Act, CCPA/CPRA, and state-specific privacy laws as applicable.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, firm name, bar number, practice areas, billing information (processed via Stripe; we do not store full credit card data).

Lead/Intake Information (processed on behalf of Subscribers): Contact details, case descriptions and incident details, medical history, symptoms, diagnoses, treatments, medical provider names and dates of service, insurance information, legal claim details and case value estimates.

AI-Generated Data: Lead quality scores, qualification assessments, medical chronologies and analyses, case merit evaluations, sentiment analysis results.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage data (IP address, browser type, device information), cookies (session, preference, analytics), and log data (server access logs, API request logs, error logs).

3.3 Information from Third Parties

CRM integrations (Filevine, Clio), web forms embedded on Subscriber websites, communication platforms (Twilio, Postmark).

4. How We Use Information

Primary Purposes: AI-powered lead qualification and scoring, medical record analysis and chronology generation, case merit assessment, CRM sync, lead intake confirmations, AI model improvement (using de-identified data only).

AI Model Training: We use de-identified data adhering to the HIPAA De-identification Standard (45 CFR § 164.514) — Expert Determination Method and Safe Harbor Method. We never use raw PHI for model training.

Secondary Purposes: Service optimization, security monitoring, fraud prevention, legal compliance, aggregated anonymized reporting.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Personal Information. LexiFlow does not sell, rent, or trade Personal Information or PHI.

Subcontractors: We share data only with essential service providers who have signed BAAs: AWS, OpenAI, Anthropic, Twilio, Stripe, and MongoDB — all with signed Business Associate Agreements.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by valid subpoena, court order, HIPAA compliance investigation, or law enforcement request (with notice to Subscriber where permitted).

6. Data Security

Technical Safeguards: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit, role-based access control (RBAC), 15-minute session timeout, immutable audit logging (6+ years), VPC isolation, WAF, DDoS protection, multi-AZ backup and DR.

Administrative Safeguards: Designated HIPAA Security Officer, annual HIPAA training, written security policies, annual risk analysis and SOC 2 Type II audit, incident response plan.

Breach Notification: In the event of a breach of unsecured PHI, LexiFlow will notify affected Subscribers within 48 hours of discovery.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Subscriber Rights (Law Firms): Access, export, or delete all PHI; request human review of AI-generated analyses; request accounting of disclosures; terminate service and request destruction of all PHI.

End User Rights (Potential Clients): Request information about data collected; request correction; request deletion; opt out of non-essential processing.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): Right to Know, Right to Delete, Right to Opt-Out, Right to Non-Discrimination, Right to Correct.

To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@lexiflow.co

10. HIPAA Compliance

LexiFlow enters into a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with each Subscriber that processes PHI through the Platform. Our BAA includes permitted uses and disclosures, prohibited uses (no sale or marketing of PHI), subcontractor requirements, 48-hour breach notification, data safeguards, return/destruction of PHI upon termination, and audit and inspection rights.

11. International Data Transfers

All LexiFlow data processing occurs within the United States (AWS US regions). International transfers comply with Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and applicable local data protection laws.

12. Children's Privacy

LexiFlow does not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that a child's information has been submitted, we will delete it promptly.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Material changes will be communicated to Subscribers via email and in-product notification at least 30 days before taking effect.
