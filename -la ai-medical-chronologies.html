[m [32m+[m[32m

How does the AI qualify leads?

LexiFlow uses reasoning AI to ask structured intake questions — case type, injury details, liability, damages — and scores each response against your firm's qualifying criteria.

[m [32m+[m[32m

Can I customize the questions?

Yes. You define the qualification rules and scoring thresholds. The AI adapts to your firm's practice areas and intake preferences.

[m [32m+[m[32m

Does it work after hours?

Absolutely. The AI Intake Agent runs 24/7. Voice AI handles phone calls when your office is closed. Leads are waiting for you in the morning.

[m [32m+[m[32m

Which CRMs are supported?

Filevine, Clio Manage, and LeadDock out of the box. Enterprise plans include custom API integrations and white-glove setup.

[m [32m+[m[32m

Is there a free trial?

Yes. 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required. Full access to the AI Intake Agent and Voice AI Receptionist.

[m [32m+[m[32m

How is my data protected?

AES-256 encryption, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, TLS 1.3 in transit. HIPAA BAA available on Enterprise plans.
