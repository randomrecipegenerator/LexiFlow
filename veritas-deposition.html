Core Forensic Capability

Contradiction Detection Engine

The engine ingests deposition transcripts, prior testimony, and all uploaded evidentiary documents, then performs structured cross-referencing to surface every inconsistency.

1

Ingest

The engine ingests deposition transcripts (PDF, text, or audio-to-text), prior testimony, and all uploaded evidentiary documents — medical records, discovery responses, police reports, surveillance footage descriptions, and more.

2

Entity Extraction

Key entities are identified across all sources: dates, times, locations, measurements, dollar amounts, procedure names, medication dosages, and witness statements about specific events.

3

Conflict Detection

Using semantic comparison, the engine compares each factual assertion against the same witness prior testimony, documented evidence, co-witness testimony, and physical impossibilities.

4

Severity Classification

Each contradiction is classified as Critical, Significant, or Minor based on how directly it undermines witness credibility.

5

Citation Generation

Every contradiction is presented with a side-by-side comparison view showing the conflicting statements and their source citations — deposition page:line, medical record page, and more.
