FAQ

Common Questions about SSD AI Software

Is LexiFlow HIPAA compliant?

Yes. LexiFlow is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with full HIPAA compliance and SOC2 Type II certification. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Can it handle hand-written medical notes?

Our advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) engine is specifically tuned for medical handwriting and low-quality faxed records common in SSD cases.

How does it help with ALJ hearings?

It provides an instant summary of "Step 3" (Listings) and "Step 4/5" (RFC) evidence, giving you a trial-ready cheat sheet to cross-examine Vocational Experts (VE).

Does it integrate with Clio or Filevine?

Yes. LexiFlow offers deep integration with Clio Grow, Clio Manage, and Filevine, allowing you to push chronologies directly to your case files.
