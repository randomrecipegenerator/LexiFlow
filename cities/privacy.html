Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026

Introduction

LexiFlow ("we," "our," or "us") provides an AI-powered legal intake platform designed for law firms. We are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the data entrusted to us by our customers (law firms) and their potential clients ("leads").

Data We Collect

  • Customer Data: Contact information, billing details, and law firm credentials.
  • Lead Data: Case narratives, personal information (name, email, phone), and legal matter details gathered during the intake process.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns to ensure platform security and performance.

How We Use Data

  • Intake Processing: Our AI agents process lead narratives to generate case summaries for law firms.
  • CRM Integration: Data is synced directly to the customer's CRM (e.g., Clio) as configured by the user.
  • Service Improvement: We use anonymized and aggregated data to improve our AI models' reasoning capabilities. We do not use identifiable client data or confidential case details to train our models without explicit consent.

Data Security and Confidentiality

  • Confidentiality: We treat all lead data as highly confidential, recognizing the sensitive nature of legal inquiries.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access Control: Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only and is logged for auditing purposes.

AI Ethics and Privacy

  • No Data Leakage: LexiFlow utilizes enterprise-grade AI models that ensure data isolation. Your firm's data is never shared with other firms or used to benefit competitors.
  • PII Masking: LexiFlow can be configured to mask or redact Personally Identifiable Information (PII) before processing if required by the firm's policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@lexiflow.co.
