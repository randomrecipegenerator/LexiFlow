End-to-End Workflow

Intelligent Automation for Clio Grow & Clio Manage

LexiFlow syncs with both parts of the Clio ecosystem, ensuring a fluid transition from the moment a lead first contacts your firm to the final settlement process.

Stage 1: Clio Grow (Lead Acquisition)

When a lead interacts with the LexiFlow Technologies Inc chat widget or calls your Voice AI Receptionist, LexiFlow evaluates their responses using deep reasoning criteria. If the case meets your parameters, LexiFlow automatically creates a Lead Profile in Clio Grow, populating standard client data fields, phone logs, and the conversation transcripts directly into the client record.

Your intake staff receives an automated notification inside Clio, ready to send the retainer agreement with one click. Retainer signed? The lead transitions instantly to Clio Manage.

Stage 2: Clio Manage (Matter Preparation)

Once the case is accepted, the legal heavy lifting begins. LexiFlow continues to support your firm by serving as your AI Medical Chronology assistant. Drag and drop thousands of pages of medical records directly into LexiFlow's dashboard. The AI parses the text, extracts treatment dates, and constructs a detailed litigation chronology.

LexiFlow automatically synchronizes these formatted timelines and medical reviews back to the corresponding Matter folder in Clio Manage, making them instantly accessible to your paralegals and trial lawyers.

Safe & Controlled Sync

Attorney-in-the-Loop Verification Framework

Law firms operate under strict ethical standards, and automated systems must never operate as a complete black box. LexiFlow features a built-in safety-valve framework that ensures a qualified human is always in control of what information is written to your official Clio records.

Before any prospect is synced, your team reviews the case details side-by-side in a staging area. From this interface, you can verify contact spellings, edit summary points, evaluate the AI-generated Case Score, and confirm that the case meets the firm's criteria. With one click, you authorize the transfer of the sanitized data into your Clio account, ensuring high-quality data integrity.

Strict Guardrails & Controls:

Data Validation: Automated checks verify that emails and phone formats are accurate to prevent database corruption in Clio.

Duplicate Detection: Checks incoming leads against existing contacts in Clio Grow using email and phone numbers to prevent duplicate files.

Granular Custom Mapping: Attorneys can manually map specific AI responses to corresponding Clio custom fields before authorizing the synchronization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answering Your Questions About Clio AI Integration

Does LexiFlow integrate with both Clio Grow and Clio Manage?

Yes. LexiFlow offers a full bidirectional integration. Qualified leads and pipeline data flow into Clio Grow to initiate retainer signing, while clinical documents and medical chronologies are synchronized directly with Matter folders inside Clio Manage.

How does LexiFlow prevent duplicate contacts in Clio?

LexiFlow automatically performs advanced duplicate check algorithms. Before any lead profiles or contacts are pushed, LexiFlow evaluates email addresses and telephone numbers against your active Clio database to highlight and merge duplicate files.

Can I map custom intake fields from LexiFlow to Clio?

Absolutely. Through our granular custom mapping dashboard, your firm can link specific AI-extracted responses directly to your standard custom fields inside Clio Manage and Clio Grow.

Is a developer required to set up the Clio integration?

No. The integration is built using OAuth 2.0 protocols. Legal administrators can connect LexiFlow and Clio Grow/Manage with a single secure login sequence, without writing any custom code.
