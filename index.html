Your Workspace FAQs

Everything you need to know about your LexiFlow Workspace environment.

What is the LexiFlow Workspace?

Your workspace is a live case management environment where AI-qualified leads, deposition contradictions, settlement models, and document drafts are pre-loaded. When you log in, you see the Rodriguez v. Mount Sinai case with a 92/100 Merit Score, 7 flagged contradictions, and $4.2M estimated trial value.

How does the AI Merit Score work?

Every lead is scored across four weighted vectors: Liability Strength (35%), Damages Severity (30%), Causation Clarity (20%), and Statute & Procedural (15%). Each vector is backed by citations from the transcript. Scores 80-100 auto-qualify; 50-79 flag for review.

How does Veritas detect contradictions?

The Contradiction Detection Engine uses a 5-step process: Ingest, Entity Extraction, Conflict Detection (semantic comparison), Severity Classification (Critical/Significant/Minor), and Citation Generation (side-by-side with source references).

Is the workspace HIPAA compliant?

Yes. We sign BAAs with firms handling PHI, maintain SOC2 Type II alignment, and provide built-in attorney-in-the-loop staging controls aligned with state bar ethics guidelines.

Which CRMs integrate with the workspace?

Native API integrations with Clio Grow, Clio Manage, Filevine, and MyCase. Sync is bidirectional: CRM updates flow back to your workspace dashboard.
