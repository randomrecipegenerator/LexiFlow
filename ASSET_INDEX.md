## Design Specs

- **Canvas Size:** 1536×1024 (16:9 widescreen) for all slides and mockups
- **Brand Colors:** Navy `#0F172A`, Teal `#0D9488`
- **Typography Strategy:** Inter (clean sans-serif) for headings/body, premium minimalist layouts
- **Style:** Elite/Enterprise — designed to impress senior law firm partners
- **Status Tags:** Green (success/approved), Amber (warning/review), Red (error/disqualified), Blue (info/reported)
