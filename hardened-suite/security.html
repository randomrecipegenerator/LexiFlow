Security & Data Protection

LexiFlow provides enterprise-grade security architecture designed to protect sensitive legal documents and HIPAA-regulated health information.

Built on a Foundation of Trust

Data Encryption

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. We utilize industry-standard key management services to ensure only authorized processes can access sensitive data.

Infrastructure Security

LexiFlow is hosted on SOC2-compliant AWS infrastructure. We employ Virtual Private Clouds (VPC), Web Application Firewalls (WAF), and automated DDoS protection to secure our network perimeter.

Access Controls

We enforce strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all internal systems. Access to client data is limited to the minimum necessary for service delivery and audit purposes.

Safe Reasoning AI

Our AI engines operate with zero-retention policies. We do not use client data or Protected Health Information (PHI) to train our models. Your proprietary firm data remains yours alone.

Continuous Monitoring

Our systems are monitored 24/7 for security anomalies. We conduct regular vulnerability scans and annual third-party penetration tests to identify and remediate potential risks.

Incident Response

We maintain a rigorous incident response plan. In the event of a suspected data breach, we commit to notifying affected clients within 48 hours, providing full transparency and mitigation steps.

Compliance & Certifications

LexiFlow undergoes rigorous independent audits to ensure we meet the highest legal and regulatory standards for data handling.
