### The Accuracy Differential

AI chronology extraction achieves **>99% accuracy on date extraction and event categorization** vs. 85–95% for manual extraction under optimal conditions. The difference comes from:

## How AI Medical Chronology Software Works

### Step 1: Document Ingestion

Upload medical records in any format — PDF, scanned images (via OCR), digital exports, or faxed documents. The system processes them all simultaneously.

**Time: seconds.**

### Step 2: Event Extraction

The AI identifies every clinical event across all documents:
- **Date of service** (admission, discharge, follow-up)
- **Diagnoses** (primary, secondary, differential)
- **Treatments** (surgery, medication, therapy)
- **Diagnostic results** (lab values, imaging findings, pathology)
- **Provider notes** (progress notes, consult notes, discharge summaries)

Each event is extracted with its source document citation.

**Time: 30–60 seconds.**

### Step 3: Chronology Assembly

Events are organized into a chronological timeline, deduplicated across providers, and formatted for readability. The output includes:

- A **summary timeline** (for demand packages and case overviews)
- A **detailed chronology** (for attorney review and expert witness preparation)
- A **gaps report** (flagging missing time periods or records)

**Time: 10–30 seconds.**

### Step 4: Attorney Review Layer

The AI flags events that need human attention:
- **Conflicting entries** (two providers documenting contradictory findings on the same date)
- **Missing standard-of-care elements** (no follow-up scheduled after abnormal finding)
- **Causation-relevant patterns** (onset of symptoms following specific events)

**Total time for complete chronology: under 3 minutes.**
