How AI Medical Chronology Software Works

Step 1: Document Ingestion

Upload medical records in any format — PDF, scanned images (via OCR), digital exports, or faxed documents.

Step 2: Event Extraction

The AI identifies every clinical event across all documents: dates of service, diagnoses, treatments, medications, labs, imaging, and provider notes. Each event is citation-backed with source document and page number. Time: 30–60 seconds.

Step 3: Chronology Assembly

Events are organized chronologically, deduplicated across providers, and formatted into a summary timeline, detailed chronology, and gaps report. Time: 10–30 seconds.

Step 4: Attorney Review Layer

AI flags conflicting entries, missing standard-of-care elements, and causation-relevant patterns. Total time: under 3 minutes.
