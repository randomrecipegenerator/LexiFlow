How the AI Intake Agent Works

From first website visit to qualified lead in your CRM — zero manual effort.

🧠

AI Lead Scoring

Reasoning AI evaluates case type, liability, jurisdiction, and damages. Every lead gets a qualification score with the nuance of a senior partner.

💬

Conversational Widget

A lightweight embeddable chatbot engages visitors naturally, collecting structured case data through conversation — not forms.

🔄

Smart Routing

Auto-assign leads to the right practice area, team member, or jurisdiction based on AI analysis of the case details.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

See every lead with qualification scores, case summaries, and conversion status. Make data-driven intake decisions instantly.

🔗

CRM Sync

Native integrations with Filevine, Clio, and LeadDock. Leads appear automatically in your existing pipeline — no data entry.

📱

SMS & Email Capture

Accept leads via SMS, email forward, or web form. LexiFlow normalizes all sources into a single structured intake record.
