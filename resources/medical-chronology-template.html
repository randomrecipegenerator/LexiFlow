Medical Chronology Template

Download a professional medical chronology template designed for personal injury and medical malpractice cases. Includes timeline, treatment summary, expense tracking, and key findings sections.

What's Included in This Template

Our medical chronology template is structured for trial readiness. Each section is designed to help paralegals and attorneys quickly identify critical medical events and build compelling case narratives.

📅 Chronological Timeline Section

Organized date-by-date format with columns for: Date, Provider/Facility, Diagnosis/Findings, Treatment/Procedure, Medications, and Next Scheduled Visit.

🔍 Key Findings Summary

Dedicated section for highlighting critical diagnoses, imaging results, specialist referrals, and deviations from standard of care.

💰 Medical Expense Tracker

Built-in table for tracking billing codes, charges, payments, insurance adjustments, and outstanding balances.

📊 Treatment Summary

High-level overview: total visits, procedures performed, medications prescribed, providers seen, and gaps in treatment.
