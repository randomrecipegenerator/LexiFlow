```

```

3. Standard of Care Check
Compares treatment against CDC, AHA, and specialty-specific protocols. Know exactly where care deviated.

4. Citation Report
Every finding backed by citations to specific records and clinical guidelines. Ready to share with expert witnesses.

5. Cost-Effective Screening
$75/case vs. $500-$2,000 for manual review. Screen 10 cases for the price of 1 manual review.

Export PDF merit reports ready for expert witness review, insurance carriers, or client consultations.
