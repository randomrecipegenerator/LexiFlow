#

How a Boutique San Francisco PI Firm Scaled Intake 3x Without Hiring

---

##

Executive Summary

**Firm:** Smith LaCien LLP
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Practice Area:** Personal Injury, Product Liability, Class Action
**Website:** smithlacien.com
**Time to Value:** 5 days
**Key Result:** 3x increase in retained cases without adding headcount

Smith LaCien LLP, a boutique plaintiff-side firm in San Francisco, needed to grow their case volume without the overhead of hiring additional intake staff. By deploying LexiFlow's AI intake alongside MeritScan for medical record review, they tripled their retained cases in 60 days while keeping their team lean.

---

##

The Challenge

Smith LaCien was facing a classic growth dilemma: more demand for their services meant more leads to screen, but each new hire added significant fixed costs.

| Metric | Before LexiFlow |
|--------|----------------|
| Average response time | 6-8 hours |
| After-hours capture | None (phone only) |
| Medical record review cost | $1,200-2,000/case |
| Case screening capacity | 15 cases/month |
| Intake staff | 2 full-time |

> *"We knew we were leaving money on the table by not responding faster, but hiring another intake paralegal would eat into our margins. We needed a smarter solution."*
> — Founding Partner, Smith LaCien LLP

##

The Solution

Smith LaCien deployed LexiFlow's AI intake suite with MeritScan integration:

1. **AI Web Intake** — Custom-branded intake form matching their green/teal brand identity
2. **AI Voice Receptionist** — 24/7 phone intake with California law awareness
3. **MeritScan** — AI medical merit review for every PI case ($75/case vs $1,500 manual)
4. **DepoLens** — Deposition conflict detection for active litigation
5. **Clio Sync** — Automatic lead push to practice management
