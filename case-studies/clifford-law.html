The Narrative

A growing Chicago firm facing an intake crisis — and how AI transformed their pipeline.

The Problem

Clifford Law Offices, a prestigious Chicago-based PI firm, was a victim of its own success. As their reputation grew, inbound leads surged — but their intake process remained manual. Response times stretched to 4-6 hours. After-hours leads went to voicemail with zero capture. Paralegals spent 60% of each day on screening rather than active casework. An estimated 40%+ of qualified leads were being lost to competitors.

The Solution

The firm deployed LexiFlow's full intake suite: AI Web Intake embedded on their website, AI Voice Receptionist for 24/7 phone screening, Smart Email Intake for inbound email parsing, MeritScan for AI medical record review, and direct CRM sync to Clio Manage. Implementation was completed in 7 days with just 2 hours of team training.

The Implementation

LexiFlow's AI was trained on Illinois law, the firm's specific case criteria, and their preferred qualification workflow. The voice AI was configured with scenario-specific screening protocols for personal injury, medical malpractice, and wrongful death — the firm's three primary practice areas. Within 48 hours, the first live lead was captured and qualified through the system.

The Results

Within 30 days, qualified intake increased 391%. Response time dropped from 4-6 hours to under 30 seconds — a 480x improvement. After-hours leads were captured for the first time, adding $8,400/month in recaptured value. Paralegal time on intake dropped from 60% to under 10% of the workday, freeing the team to focus on active litigation.
