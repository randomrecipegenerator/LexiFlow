Our AI screens incoming leads for primary liability criteria and injury severity. As soon as a lead is qualified, LexiFlow pushes the client profile into Filevine Lead Docket. Transcripts, geolocation data, and case narratives are instantly mapped into custom Lead Docket fields.

Your intake agents can trigger an automatic text or email containing your digital retainer, allowing you to sign cases within minutes of initial contact.

Once the lead is converted to an active Filevine Project, LexiFlow's medical analysis suite kicks in. Drag-and-drop clinical records directly into LexiFlow to compile dynamic medical chronologies. The AI identifies inconsistencies, medical errors, and pre-existing conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answering Your Questions About Filevine AI Integration

Does LexiFlow support custom layouts inside Filevine?

Yes. LexiFlow is built to accommodate the highly customizable nature of Filevine. Our API connector allows you to map structured extraction variables directly to your firm's specific custom layout fields, including multi-select fields and customs tables.

How is intake data pushed to Filevine?

Once a lead is qualified, LexiFlow pushes the payload directly into Filevine or Lead Docket using official API endpoints. This triggers the automatic generation of a new Project, populates the Intake Section, and uploads associated intake summaries or call logs.

Does LexiFlow support document mapping in Filevine?

Absolutely. AI-extracted medical summaries, PDF chronologies, and transcript sheets are automatically organized and posted to the "Docs" tab within the newly initialized Filevine Project under designated folders.

How secure is the API connection between LexiFlow and Filevine?

LexiFlow uses enterprise-grade OAuth 2.0 authentication to connect to your Filevine environment. All data synchronized is fully encrypted using TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest, backed by our HIPAA and SOC2 compliance programs.
