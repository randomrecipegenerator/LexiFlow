### The Challenge

**The Problem:** Like many successful PI firms, Clifford Law was a victim of its own growth. As their reputation grew, so did inbound leads — but their intake process remained manual.

| Metric | Before LexiFlow |
|--------|----------------|
| Average response time | 4-6 hours |
| After-hours capture | 0% (voicemail only) |
| Lead qualification time | 15-20 minutes per lead |
| Missed leads (est.) | 40%+ |
| Paralegal time on intake | 60% of workday |

> *"We were losing cases we should have won — simply because we couldn't get to everyone fast enough. Our paralegals were spending more time screening leads than working on active cases."*
> — Managing Partner, Clifford Law Offices

### The Solution: LexiFlow Reasoning AI

Clifford Law deployed LexiFlow's full intake suite across their Chicago headquarters:

1. **AI Web Intake** — Embedded intake form on cliffordlaw.com that captures 100+ data points
2. **AI Voice Receptionist** — 24/7 phone intake that handles screening, scheduling, and qualification
3. **Smart Email Intake** — Automatic parsing and qualification of every inbound email lead
4. **MeritScan Integration** — AI medical record review for quick case valuation
5. **CRM Sync** — Direct push to Clio Manage for immediate attorney assignment
