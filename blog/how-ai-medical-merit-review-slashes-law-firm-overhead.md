**Opportunity cost of delayed intake:** Every day a meritorious case sits in the review queue is a day the statute of limitations ticks closer. For a $1M case where each month of delay reduces settlement value by 5%, one week of screening delay costs $12,500 in settlement leverage.

**Cost of inconsistent standards:** Different paralegals apply different heuristics. One might flag a deviation as critical; another might miss it entirely. AI applies the same standard to every case.
