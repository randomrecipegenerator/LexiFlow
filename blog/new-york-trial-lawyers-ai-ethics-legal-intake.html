AI Implementation for New York Medical Malpractice Firms

New York's legal landscape requires stringent adherence to the Rules of Professional Conduct, especially regarding attorney advertising and client communication.

Rule 1.18: Duties to Prospective Clients

In New York, attorneys owe a duty of confidentiality to prospective clients who provide information during intake. LexiFlow’s platform is architected to silo prospective client data, ensuring that NY firms remain compliant with Rule 1.18 even during high-volume screening.

Supervision of AI Systems

Per NY ethics guidance, partners must make reasonable efforts to ensure that the firm has in effect measures giving reasonable assurance that the AI's conduct is compatible with the professional obligations of the lawyer.

NY Medical Malpractice Intake Nuance

LexiFlow’s AI is specifically tuned for the 'Certificate of Merit' requirements in NY medical malpractice cases, identifying key deviations from the standard of care in medical records to assist NY attorneys in their initial evaluations.
