## The Full LexiFlow Suite

LexiFlow 1.0 ships with all modules active and production-hardened:

### AI Intake Widget
24/7 lead capture and qualification. The widget engages website visitors the moment they show intent, conducts a natural-language intake conversation, and scores each lead for merit, practice area fit, and urgency — all in under 30 seconds.

### Voice AI Intake
Patients call your firm after hours? Voice AI handles the initial intake conversation, captures case details, and routes hot leads to the on-call attorney — automatically, with HIPAA-compliant call recording.

### MeritScan — Medical Merit Review
Upload medical records and get a citation-backed preliminary merit report in under 3 minutes. The AI extracts diagnoses, treatments, dates, and timestamps; cross-references them against standard-of-care guidelines; and produces a structured report that tells you immediately whether the case has merit.

### Medical Chronologies
Automated treatment timelines with >99% extraction accuracy. Build complete, citation-backed chronologies from 5,000+ pages of records in under 60 seconds.

### Veritas Deposition™ — Deposition Intelligence
Upload deposition transcripts and get issue-organized digests with every contradiction flagged. Cross-reference testimony against medical records, prior depositions, and expert reports. Generate issue-specific digests for trial preparation.

### Narrative Synthesis
Draft demand letters, case summaries, and settlement brochures from the AI-extracted case data — consistent, citation-backed, and ready for attorney review.

### CRM Integrations
Bi-directional sync with Filevine and Clio. Leads flow from intake directly into your case management system. Case data flows back into LexiFlow for document processing.

## Security and Compliance

Every LexiFlow module was built with compliance as a foundational requirement, not an afterthought.

- **HIPAA compliance:** Encrypted data transmission, audit logging, BAA execution, role-based access controls
- **SOC2 readiness:** Access controls, data segregation, incident response procedures
- **Data retention:** Configurable retention policies aligned with state bar requirements
- **AI audit trails:** Every AI action is logged and attributable for ethics review
- **Jurisdictional enforcement:** State-specific rules applied automatically (certificate of merit, expert report deadlines, statute of limitations)
