## The Deposition Digest Bottleneck

You just finished a 6-hour deposition. The transcript lands in your inbox at 5 PM. Your partner needs a digest by 9 AM tomorrow.

In most firms, someone pulls an all-nighter. A paralegal reads 180+ pages of testimony, highlights key passages, cross-references medical records, flags contradictions, and types up a summary — all before sunrise.

The average deposition takes **4–6 hours of digest time per hour of testimony**. A single 6-hour deposition consumes **24–36 hours of manual work**. For a firm taking 30 depositions per month, that's **720–1,080 hours** — the equivalent of a full-time paralegal working exclusively on deposition digest.

The bottleneck isn't strategy. It's digestion.

This guide presents a new workflow: **how to digest a deposition in 15 minutes** using AI-assisted deposition analysis — without sacrificing accuracy or depth.

## The 15-Minute AI-Assisted Deposition Digest Workflow

The following workflow is designed for Veritas Deposition™ by LexiFlow, but the principles apply to any AI-assisted deposition analysis tool.

### Minute 0-1: Upload and Process

**Manual equivalent:** 30 minutes (locating transcript file, opening, scanning for format issues)

**AI-assisted:** Upload the transcript file. Veritas Deposition™ accepts ASCII, PDF, and Word formats from any court reporter. The AI parses the Q&A structure, identifies all speakers (often 3–6 per deposition), and builds a structured data model of the entire transcript in under 60 seconds.

**What to do:** Drag and drop the transcript. Verify speaker labels are correct. Click "Process."

### Minute 1-3: Review Executive Summary

**Manual equivalent:** 2–3 hours (first full read-through)

**AI-assisted:** Veritas Deposition™ generates an executive summary that includes:

- **Case name and deponent** — auto-extracted from transcript
- **Key issues identified** — liability, damages, causation, credibility grouped by topic
- **Critical testimony highlights** — the 5–10 most important exchanges flagged
- **Contradiction count** — number of inconsistencies found vs. prior testimony, medical records, and expert reports
- **Witness credibility indicators** — evasions, inconsistencies, demeanor notes
- **Exhibit correlation** — each critical testimony point linked to specific exhibits

**What to do:** Read the executive summary. This replaces the first full read-through. Note any issues you want to drill into during the review phase.

### Minute 3-7: Drill into Prioritized Issues

**Manual equivalent:** 2–3 hours (issue coding and organization)

**AI-assisted:** Veritas Deposition™ organizes testimony into issue-specific digests. Instead of reading chronologically, you read by legal topic:

- **Liability digest** — all testimony related to fault, duty, breach, causation
- **Damages digest** — testimony on injuries, medical treatment, pain and suffering, economic loss
- **Credibility digest** — inconsistencies, evasions, prior inconsistent statements
- **Medical cross-reference digest** — every medical claim in testimony linked to source records

**What to do:** Open the Liability digest first (for plaintiff-side cases) or Damages digest (for demand letter preparation). Review flagged testimony. Each highlight is clickable — it takes you to the exact page and line in the full transcript for context.

### Minute 7-10: Review Contradictions and Generate Cross-Examination Questions

**Manual equivalent:** 3–5 hours (comparing testimony against records, drafting questions)

**AI-assisted:** The Contradiction Detection Engine™ has already cross-referenced the deposition against:
- Prior deposition transcripts in the case
- Medical records and treatment notes
- Expert reports and opinions
- Interrogatory responses and admissions

Each contradiction is presented side-by-side:

| Testimony | Evidence | Delta |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| "I never said there were complications" | Operative note: "Procedure complicated by..." | Direct contradiction |
| "Patient was stable overnight" | qSOFA score of 2 at triage | 10% mortality risk = not stable |
| "Antibiotics were started at 19:45" | MAR: No administration until 10:15 | 14-hour gap |

For each contradiction, Veritas Deposition™ generates an impeachment question:

> "Doctor, you testified that antibiotics were started at 19:45. The hospital's own Medication Administration Record shows no antibiotics were administered for 14 hours after that time. Is the MAR incorrect?"

**What to do:** Review the contradiction panel. Copy the top 3–5 impeachment questions into your cross-examination outline. Each comes with the exact page/line citation for trial use.

### Minute 10-12: Medical Record Cross-Reference

**Manual equivalent:** 2–4 hours (pulling records, comparing, verifying)

**AI-assisted:** Every medical reference in the deposition testimony is automatically linked to the source medical record:

- "Patient had a fever of 103.2" → linked to ER Triage notes, page 1
- "X-ray showed no fracture" → linked to Radiology Report, exhibit D-12
- "I prescribed Vancomycin" → linked to MAR entry at 19:15

**What to do:** Spot-check 3–5 medical cross-references. Each link opens the source document in Discovery-Vault™ for full context. If all cross-references check out, you're done in 2 minutes.

### Minute 12-14: Generate Issue-Specific Digests

**Manual equivalent:** 2–3 hours (writing, formatting, proofreading)

**AI-assisted:** Veritas Deposition™ produces tailored digests for each use case:

- **Full narrative digest** — chronological summary for the case file
- **Issue digests** — liability, damages, causation, credibility — each as a standalone document
- **Cross-examination outline** — contradiction-focused questions with citations
- **Mediation summary** — condensed digest optimized for settlement discussions

**What to do:** Select the digests you need. Export to PDF or Word. The partner gets the Liability digest and Cross-examination outline. The demand letter team gets the Damages digest and Mediation summary.

### Minute 14-15: Final Review and Distribution

**Manual equivalent:** 1 hour (proofreading, formatting, distributing)

**AI-assisted:** Review the generated digests for completeness. Verify that all critical issues were captured. Distribute to the case team via the dashboard or CRM sync.

**What to do:** Quick scan of each digest. Click "Sync to Filevine" or "Share with Team." Done.
