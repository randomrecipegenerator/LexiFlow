How AI Medical Merit Review Cuts Case Screening from 8 Hours to 3 Minutes

📅 June 1, 2026 ⏱ 11 min read 🏷 AI medical merit review 🔒 HIPAA Compliant

Every plaintiff-side attorney knows the feeling: a stack of medical records lands on your desk from a potential new client. It could be a life-changing case — or a dead end. Someone needs to read every page, flag every deviation from the standard of care, and build a chronology of treatment. That someone is usually a paralegal billing $150–$250 an hour, spending 8 to 12 hours on a single case.

Now multiply that by 20 to 50 leads per week. $12,000 to $18,000 in unbilled labor per week just to separate viable cases from dead ends. And studies show manual screening misses up to 60% of meritorious medical malpractice cases.

Enter AI medical merit review — a technology that fundamentally changes the economics of case screening.

How AI Medical Merit Review Works

Step 1: Document Ingestion & OCR

Upload PDFs, scans, or digital exports. OCR converts image-based documents to searchable text in seconds.

Step 2: Chronology Extraction

AI extracts every clinical event and builds a structured medical timeline in under 60 seconds.

Step 3: Standard of Care Analysis

AI compares care against medical standards — diagnostic protocols, treatment timelines, referral patterns — and identifies deviations as citation-backed findings.

Step 4: Causation Assessment

Traces the causal chain from deviation through harm to damages.

Step 5: Merit Scoring & Report

Outputs merit score (1–100), liability score, damages estimate, risk factors. Total time: 2–3 minutes.
