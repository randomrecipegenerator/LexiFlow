## Four Modules, One Platform

The Enterprise Suite adds four specialized modules to the LexiFlow platform, each targeting a specific pain point in the litigation workflow.

### Discovery-Vault™ — Automated Document Intelligence

Discovery-Vault processes and analyzes discovery documents at scale — thousands of pages in minutes rather than weeks. The module uses reasoning AI to:

- **Identify responsive documents** with context-aware relevance scoring
- **Flag privilege and confidentiality** issues against firm-defined criteria
- **Extract key facts, dates, and parties** into structured data
- **Generate privilege logs** automatically from review decisions
- **Create issue-specific document collections** for deposition prep or trial

Instead of associates spending 10–15 hours per week on document review, Discovery-Vault completes initial review in under two hours per 1,000 documents. The associate's role shifts from reading every page to auditing AI-flagged documents and exercising judgment on privilege calls.

### Settlement-Predictor Pro™ — Data-Driven Case Valuation

Settlement-Predictor Pro applies predictive modeling against a corpus of anonymized settlement data, firm-specific case history, and real-time jurisdictional analytics to:

- **Generate settlement ranges** with confidence intervals based on case type, jurisdiction, and damages
- **Factor in liability strength** using LexiFlow's MeritScan scoring
- **Adjust for venue-specific trends** (jury verdict histories, judge assignment patterns)
- **Project timeline-to-resolution** with monthly probability curves
- **Provide negotiation anchors** based on comparable closed cases

The tool does not replace attorney judgment — it augments it with data. Attorneys who use settlement analytics in mediation report 18–25% higher recovery rates compared to intuition-based evaluation alone.

### Veritas Deposition™ — Real-Time Deposition Intelligence

Veritas Deposition™ transforms deposition preparation and execution. The module ingests deposition transcripts, prior testimony, and medical records to:

- **Flag contradictions** between current testimony and prior statements or records
- **Identify expert witness conflicts** against a national database of expert testimony
- **Generate real-time question suggestions** based on witness answers
- **Cross-reference medical records** against testimony for accuracy verification
- **Produce instant deposition summaries** organized by issue, witness, and timeline

For firms that take 20+ depositions per month, Veritas Deposition™ saves an estimated 40–50 hours of preparation and summary time — while catching inconsistencies that human reviewers miss.

### Compliance-Shield™ — Ethics and Regulatory Guardrails

Compliance-Shield ensures that every AI-assisted action within the LexiFlow platform stays within ethical and regulatory boundaries. Features include:

- **Automated conflict checking** against firm client databases
- **Jurisdictional rule enforcement** (e.g., certificate of merit requirements, expert report deadlines)
- **AI output audit trails** for ethics review and client transparency
- **Data retention policy enforcement** aligned with state bar requirements
- **Client communication logging** for supervision and review

Compliance-Shield addresses the single most common concern legal ethics committees raise about AI adoption: the ability to supervise and audit AI-assisted work product.
