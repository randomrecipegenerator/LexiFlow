How to Digest a Deposition in 15 Minutes with AI

📅 June 22, 2026 ⏱ 10 min read Professional Module

The 30-Hour Paralegal Bottleneck

Every trial attorney has faced this scenario: a critical witness deposition finishes on a Thursday, and by Monday morning, you need a complete, organized summary. Traditional manual deposition digests are excruciatingly slow. For every single hour of transcript, a legal assistant or paralegal spends 4 to 6 hours reviewing, coding, and summarizing. For a six-hour deposition, that equates to 24 to 36 hours of manual typing.

For plaintiff-side contingency firms, this represents a massive, non-billable overhead cost. For defense-side firms, it leads to heavily scrutinized invoices. Either way, it's a bottleneck that slows down trial prep.

Step-by-Step: Transforming Raw Transcripts to Partner Outlines in 15 Minutes

Reasoning AI platforms have completely revolutionized how firms approach deposition summaries. Here is the exact, streamlined 3-step process to digest a raw deposition in 15 minutes using LexiFlow's Veritas Deposition™ engine:

Step 1: Raw File Upload and Speaker Alignment

Upload your ASCII text transcript, PDF, or Word file directly into the secure, HIPAA-compliant dashboard. In less than 60 seconds, LexiFlow's parser processes the transcript, aligning speakers, stripping court reporter formatting, and indexing page-and-line numbers.

Step 2: Semantic Issue Mapping

Define your core trial issues (e.g., standard of care deviations, symptom onset, pre-existing conditions). Instead of performing standard keyword searches, LexiFlow's vector database analyzes the semantic intent of the text, automatically grouping relevant testimony under the appropriate issue headings.

Step 3: Verification & Export

Every single summary block produced contains clickable, cited page-and-line numbers. Click any summary line to display the original raw context. Once verified, export your outline as a pre-formatted Word document or sync it straight to your case files in Clio, Filevine, or MyCase.
