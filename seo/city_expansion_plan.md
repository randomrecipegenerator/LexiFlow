## 4. State-Specific Legal Nuances (for Content Team)
To ensure high conversion, each page must mention local legal facts:
-   **Pennsylvania:** Certificate of Merit requirements, MCARE Fund.
-   **Illinois:** Affidavit of Merit (Section 2-622), Cook County court nuances.
-   **Florida:** Pre-suit investigation requirements, sovereign immunity caps.
-   **Texas:** Tort reform caps (Chapter 74), expert report deadlines.
-   **California:** MICRA caps (recently updated), CCP § 364 notice requirements.
