# SEO Keyword Audit & Strategy Update - June 2026

## 1. Current Content Audit
Our existing blog content (14+ articles) provides a strong foundation in the following areas:
- **AI Medical Chronologies:** High coverage of accuracy, process, and case valuation.
- **AI Legal Intake:** Strong authority on ethics, lead leakage, and ROI.
- **Product Launches:** Good introductory content for DepoLens and the Enterprise Suite.
- **State-Specific SEO:** Dedicated guides for PA, NY, and IL.

**Gaps Identified:**
- **Niche MedMal Leads:** We lack dedicated content for specific, high-value MedMal categories like surgical errors or birth injuries.
- **Transactional "Last Mile" Keywords:** While we mention document drafting, we aren't targeting high-intent terms for settlement demand automation.
- **Discovery Lifecycle:** Our "Discovery-Vault" product needs more educational content targeting terms like "document indexing" and "litigation review."

---

## 2. Suggested High-Intent Keywords (New Opportunities)

### Keyword 1: "settlement demand software"
- **Target Audience:** PI Law Firm Partners and Operations Managers.
- **Intent:** Transactional/Commercial.
- **Content Brief:**
    - **Working Title:** "From Chronology to Cash: How Settlement Demand Software Accelerates PI Firm Revenue"
    - **Target Keyword:** settlement demand software
    - **Secondary Keywords:** demand letter automation, PI case management automation, automated demand packages.
    - **Brief:** Focus on the bottleneck of drafting demand letters. Explain how LexiFlow’s Auto-Doc Drafter pulls data directly from medical chronologies to generate professional, data-backed demand letters in minutes. Emphasize consistency and reduced error rates.

### Keyword 2: "surgical error intake automation"
- **Target Audience:** Medical Malpractice Specialist Attorneys.
- **Intent:** Niche/High-Intent.
- **Content Brief:**
    - **Working Title:** "The Specialist's Advantage: Why Surgical Error Cases Require Advanced Intake Automation"
    - **Target Keyword:** surgical error intake automation
    - **Secondary Keywords:** surgical error lead qualification, medical malpractice screening software, anesthesia error intake.
    - **Brief:** Highlight the medical complexity of surgical error leads. Detail how LexiFlow’s Reasoning AI asks dynamic, medically-informed questions (e.g., about anesthesia logs or informed consent) to filter high-merit cases instantly, reducing the burden on senior partners.

### Keyword 3: "medical record review for litigation"
- **Target Audience:** Litigation Teams and Mass Tort Practice Leads.
- **Intent:** Commercial/Educational.
- **Content Brief:**
    - **Working Title:** "Winning Discovery: The Modern Attorney's Guide to Medical Record Review for Litigation"
    - **Target Keyword:** medical record review for litigation
    - **Secondary Keywords:** AI document indexing for law firms, automated discovery review, high-volume record analysis.
    - **Brief:** Position LexiFlow as a critical tool for the discovery phase of complex litigation. Explain how the combination of Discovery-Vault and MeritScan allows firms to index, search, and analyze thousands of pages of medical evidence, finding "smoking gun" admissions that manual review often misses.

---

## 3. Strategic Recommendations
- **Lead Magnets:** Leverage the existing "Medical Chronology Template" and "Record Review Checklist" resources by linking to them prominently in these new high-intent posts.
- **Internal Linking:** Map the "Surgical Error" post to `/solutions.html` and the "Settlement Demand" post to the "Auto-Doc Drafter" product section.
- **Case Study Integration:** Use snippets from the Clifford Law or Smith LaCien case studies to provide "Proof of Concept" in the new litigation-focused content.
