# Competitor Analysis: MeritScan & DepoLens

This report analyzes the top direct competitors for our niche legal AI products to help refine our sales strategy and handle objections.

---

## 1. MeritScan (AI Medical Merit Review)
*Target: Personal Injury & Medical Malpractice Firms*

| Competitor | Pricing (Est.) | Key Features | Marketing Angle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **EvenUp** | $500 - $1,500 / case | Demand letters, medical chronologies, case valuation. | "Maximize settlement value with AI-driven demands." |
| **Wisedocs** | Usage/Page-based | Rapid OCR, indexing, and clinical summarization. | "The fastest medical record platform for legal/insurance." |
| **Torticity** | Per case / Service-based | Medical record review, mass tort qualification. | "Expert-led, AI-powered medical record analysis." |
| **Casepeer AI** | Part of $100+/mo sub | Integrated summarization within Case Management. | "The power of AI directly in your case management." |
| **Clearbrief** | $150 - $250 / mo | Fact-checking records against briefs, instant links. | "AI that never hallucinates; verify every medical fact." |

### MeritScan's Competitive Edge:
*   **Specialization**: Unlike EvenUp (Demands) or Wisedocs (Indexing), MeritScan focuses specifically on **Negligence Markers** (breach of standard of care).
*   **Cost**: Our $75 'Preliminary Merit Report' is significantly cheaper than EvenUp's $500+ demands or human Legal Nurse reviews ($1,500+).

## 2. DepoLens (AI Deposition Conflict Detection)
*Target: Litigation Firms*

| Competitor | Pricing (Est.) | Key Features | Marketing Angle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CoCounsel (TR)** | ~$250 / mo per user | Depo summaries, research, document review. | "Your first AI legal assistant." |
| **Liquid Litigation** | Subscription-based | Fact extraction, chronology, litigation management. | "Intelligent litigation management at scale." |
| **Verbit (Captell)** | Per minute + AI Fee | Transcription + GenAI summaries/insights. | "Turn transcripts into actionable legal insights." |
| **Lexis+ AI** | High Enterprise Sub | Transcript analysis, global legal research integration. | "Unmatched legal authority meets generative AI." |
| **LitiGate** | Subscription-based | Litigation lifecycle automation, transcript analysis. | "Automate the tedious parts of litigation." |

### DepoLens's Competitive Edge:
*   **Conflict-First**: Most competitors focus on 'Summarization'. DepoLens focuses on **Witness Conflict Detection** (pointing out where Witness A contradicts Witness B).
*   **Long Context**: Using Gemini 1.5 Pro's 2M window allows us to analyze **all** depositions in a case at once, whereas RAG-based tools (like earlier CoCounsel versions) might miss nuanced cross-transcript conflicts.

---

## Refined Sales Pitch
*   **Against EvenUp**: "EvenUp is great for demands, but MeritScan is your 'early warning system' to screen out bad MedMal cases before you waste $2,000 on an expert."
*   **Against CoCounsel**: "CoCounsel is a generalist. DepoLens is a specialist tool that specifically hunts for witness contradictions across your entire case file, not just one transcript at a time."
